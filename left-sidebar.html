Hidden Layers: AI & Design Conference 2024

Hosted by the Köln International School of Design , focusing on AI in design. Organized by KITeGG, the event explores 4AI's aesthetics, biases, and impact through talks, workshops, and exhibitions.

  • https://hiddenlayers.de/

Motion Design System

In the "Hidden Layers" project, our team developed a cohesive motion design system for the 2024 Design & AI Conference. We reinterpreted the institution’s corporate identity to create a flexible design framework, setting clear guidelines for visual elements, motion, and composition. My primary role was in the conceptual phase, where I helped shape the overall look and feel by defining the visual rules, including the maximum and minimum properties for all design components.

Production

During production, I focused on creating generative background visuals based on these elements, establishing the foundation for the conference’s visual identity. I also contributed as a creative director, overseeing the organization and coordination of the production process to ensure consistency and timely delivery.
