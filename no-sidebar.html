NEW NOW Festivall 2023

NEW NOW is a leading European festival for digital art. In its second edition, held from June 1 to August 6, 2023, at the UNESCO World Heritage Site Zeche Zollverein. It became a hub for showcasing and creating digital artworks. The festival, themed "Hypernatural Forces," explored the interplay between nature and digital technology, blurring the boundaries between reality and simulation.

MXL#15: SOUNDS OF SILENCE

IMXL#15 SOUNDS OF SILENCE, led by Darsha Hewitt, focused on the socio-ecological impact of sound, combining theoretical research with hands-on experimentation in sound and technology.

Production

The project unfolded through several phases. Initially, we conducted sound walks, captured sound sculptures, and explored sound sustainability. The midterm phase focused on developing a concept paper and moved into an intense production phase, building and programming the prototype sound machine.

We presented the prototype at the NEW NOW Festival in Essen. We performed with custom-built mechanical suits, real-time visuals, and sound manipulation via a rotary speaker and tape delay system. After the festival, I focused mainly on the audioreactive real time animation. We re-evaluated the machine, evolved its design and functionality before the final showcase at KISD Parcour.
