About Me

I’m Paul Kimmerl, an Integrated Design student at Cologne International School of Design (KISD), with a deep passion for exploring the intersection of technology and creativity. Specializing in 3D graphics, interaction design, and immersive media, I thrive on pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in digital art and design. My work centers around combining generative visuals, spatial audio, and real-time interactive experiences, seamlessly blending technical innovation with artistic expression.
